About Charity Rosewell

We are a non-profit organization committed to creating lasting change in communities around the world.

Our Mission & Vision

Founded in 2010, Charity Rosewell has been at the forefront of humanitarian efforts, providing essential support to vulnerable communities across 25 countries.

Our mission is to empower individuals and communities to overcome poverty, injustice, and inequality. We believe in a world where every person has the opportunity to thrive and reach their full potential.

Through our innovative programs and strategic partnerships, we've impacted over 2 million lives, providing access to education, healthcare, clean water, and economic opportunities.

We operate with transparency, accountability, and efficiency, ensuring that 92% of every dollar donated goes directly to our programs and beneficiaries.
