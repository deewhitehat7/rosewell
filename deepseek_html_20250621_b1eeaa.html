Our Cloning Services

BACPAC Genomics

Distributes BAC, PAC, and fosmid clones from genomic libraries developed at Roswell Park.

  • Human, mouse, canine, porcine, and zebrafish libraries
  • Clones shipped as agar stabs
  • Commercial licensing available
Ordering:

Submit requests via bacpacresources.org

Contact:

Orders: bacpacorders@bacpacresources.org

Technical: pieterjdejong@bacpacresources.org

Gene Modulation Services

Comprehensive resources for gene knockdown and overexpression studies.

  • shRNA Libraries (human/mouse)
  • CRISPRi/a pooled libraries
  • Full-length human cDNA clones
Ordering:

Email renae.holtz@roswellpark.org with gene names and clone IDs

Consultations:

Available for experimental design

Genomics Shared Resource

Advanced genomic analysis services and sample processing.

  • DNA/RNA extraction from multiple sources
  • Next-generation sequencing (WGS, RNA-seq)
  • Single-cell sequencing
Sample Requirements:

Cells: 250K–1 million cells

Tissue: 10–25 mg frozen

Contact:

Dr. Prashant Singh: Prashant.Singh@RoswellPark.org
